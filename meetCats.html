Companion Animals Act & NSW Companion

Animal Register

The Companion Animals Act was passed by the NSW Government in 1998. Under this Act all kittens born after 1 July 1999 and kept as pets must be permanently identified through micro-chipping by12 weeks of age, and must be regis-tered through alocal council on the NSW Companion Animals Register by 6 months of age. Cats born earlier who change ownership must also be chipped and registered. All UNSW campus cats have been microchipped and registered as owned by CCNSW through Randwick City Council
